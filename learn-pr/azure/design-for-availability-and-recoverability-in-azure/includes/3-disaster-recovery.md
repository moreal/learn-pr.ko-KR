### <a name="risk-assessment-and-process-inventory"></a><span data-ttu-id="e72c7-118">위험 평가 및 프로세스 인벤토리</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e72c7-118">Risk assessment and process inventory</span></span>
